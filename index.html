About Formula 1

The Pinnacle of Motorsport

Formula 1 represents the highest level of motorsport, combining cutting-edge technology, incredible driver skill, and high-speed thrills. It's more than a race; it's an engineering marvel.

Global Spectacle

With races hosted in iconic circuits worldwide, Formula 1 captures the essence of speed, innovation, and global competition. Each track offers unique challenges, from tight street circuits to high-speed straights.

Drivers and Teams

Formula 1 is home to legendary drivers and teams. From the high-tech factories to the pit wall strategies, every element contributes to the battle for the championship.

Feel the Adrenaline

Experience the roaring engines, daring overtakes, and split-second decisions that define every F1 race. This is the ultimate test of speed and precision.

Formula 1 Technology

Hybrid Power Units

Modern F1 cars use advanced hybrid engines that combine traditional combustion power with electric energy recovery systems for unmatched efficiency and performance.

Aerodynamics

From the front wing to the rear diffuser, every curve and surface is optimized for maximum downforce and minimal drag. Aerodynamics are key to lap-time gains.

Advanced Tires

Formula 1 tires are engineered for extreme performance. Drivers manage degradation and grip, with strategies often revolving around tire choices.
